FAQ

Robinhood Chain · pigs · $OINK

What are PixelPigs?

3,333 pixel-art pigs on Robinhood Chain. Pure code — no AI. Each pig is an ERC-721 NFT you can hold and trade on OpenSea.

Mint at hoodpixelpigs.xyz · max 11 pigs per wallet · price targets ~$1 USD in ETH (set on-chain).

How do I add Robinhood Chain to MetaMask?

  • Open MetaMask → network dropdown → Add network
  • Or tap ADD ROBINHOOD CHAIN on the mint page

Use these settings:

  • Network name: Robinhood Chain
  • RPC URL: https://rpc.mainnet.chain.robinhood.com
  • Chain ID: 4663
  • Currency: ETH
  • Explorer: https://robinhoodchain.blockscout.com

You need a small amount of ETH on Robinhood Chain for mint gas + mint price. Bridge ETH to chain 4663 before minting.

Which wallet can I use?

Any EVM wallet on chain 4663: MetaMask, OKX, Rabby, Robinhood Wallet, etc. On mint and $OINK pages, tap CONNECT and pick your wallet from the list.

What is $OINK?

$OINK is the PixelPigs community token on Robinhood Chain. Fixed supply: 335,700,000 OINK. Trading opens after mint out (all 3,333 pigs minted).

Token: 0xF2c5…745b

Are pigs transferable?

Yes. PixelPigs are full ERC-721 NFTs — trade, send, list on OpenSea anytime.

Why does mint price show more or less than $1?

Mint price is set in ETH on-chain. The site converts live ETH/USD so it targets ~$1. When ETH moves, the dollar estimate can drift — refresh the mint page to see the current USD equivalent.
